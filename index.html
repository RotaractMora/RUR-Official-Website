Are You Ready? 2019 | Sessions

Webinar Series

A series of web seminars on career paths that undergraduates can follow after their graduation.

Résumé Centre

Provides the undergraduates with the opportunity to get their CVs checked by industry experts.

Heads Up

A session on making quality résumés and facing interviews with confidence as these two aspects play a vital role in a job recruitment process.

The Career Coach

A session that concentrates on corporate expectations, challenges and adapting to the business world after graduation.

The Image

A session where a celebrity would be providing awareness on personal branding and its importance when moving forward in this competitive world.

Youth BIZ

A panel discussion on entrepreneurship and initiating startups to encourage the enthusiastic undergraduates who think out of the box.

Flagship Day

The limelight of ‘Are You Ready? 2019’ where mock and real interviews would be conducted for the final year undergraduates to support them achieve their dream job.
